Data Is Everywhere

Who I am?

Hi, I’m Shashwat — a curious mind who finds meaning in data and stories behind every number. I’m a Microsoft certified data analyst who believes that data isn’t just numbers on a screen — it’s potential waiting to be unlocked. Whether I’m building interactive dashboards or revealing patterns hidden in raw information, I love turning complexity into clarity. But what truly sets me apart? It’s my curiosity. I constantly ask “why,” explore deeper, and seek insights that lead to better decisions. Tools like Power BI and Excel are just the beginning — I bring a mindset of continuous learning, creative problem-solving, and a love for storytelling. Outside the data world, I dive into content creation and visual storytelling, which gives me a fresh, human approach to presenting insights. For me, data isn’t just about charts — it’s about connection. It should inform, inspire, and engage. I’m excited to collaborate, grow, and create impact through the lens of data.
